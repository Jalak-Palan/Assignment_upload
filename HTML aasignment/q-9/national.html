New Education policy Announced

The Union Cabinet, chaired by the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has approved the New Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) which aims to transform the education system in India. The policy focuses on holistic development, critical thinking, and creativity among students. It emphasizes the importance of early childhood care and education, vocational training, and the integration of technology in education. The NEP 2020 also aims to make education more inclusive and accessible to all sections of society.

Floods in Assam

Assam has been grappling with severe floods due to heavy monsoon rains, leading to widespread devastation in the region. The floods have affected millions of people, displacing families and causing significant damage to infrastructure, homes, and agricultural land. Relief operations are underway, with the government and various organizations working to provide food, shelter, and medical aid to the affected population. Efforts are also being made to assess the damage and plan for rehabilitation and reconstruction in the flood-hit areas.
